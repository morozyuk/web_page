Ivan Franko National University of Lviv is a classical higher educational establishment with powerful scientific schools, old traditions and modern innovation approaches.
 Its principal aim is to provide high educational and scientific standards, cultural, social needs of a person, the society and state in the process of training highly qualified specialists.
The University activity is formed on the grounds of preserving Ukrainian culture and traditions, developing national consciousness and identity.
