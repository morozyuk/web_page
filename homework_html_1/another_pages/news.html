AT LVIV UNIVERSITY, MINISTER OF EDUCATION AND SCIENCE OF UKRAINE TELLS ABOUT LANGUAGE ISSUE IN LAW ON EDUCATION

On October 26, 2017, a round table discussion “Language Issue in the Law on Education” was held in the Mirror Hall of the Ivan Franko National University of Lviv.
Minister of Education and Science of Ukraine, Liliya Hrynevych, Rector of Lviv University, Professor Volodymyr Melnyk, representatives of the Polish national minority and schools with Polish as a language of instruction took part in the event.
